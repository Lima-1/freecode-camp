Dr. Kwame Nkrumah

The man who gained independence for Ghana

Dr. Nkrumah from right, with the other big six declaring independence on March 1957.

Here's a brief biography of Dr. Kwame Nkrumah:

  • 1909 - Born in Nkoroful, Ghana
  • 1925 - Became a student teacher at his elementary school
  • 1930 - Obtained Teacher's certificate from Prince of Wales Collage at Achimota.
  • 1931 - Posted to Roman Catholic Primary at Elmina and there started involving in politics, formed Nzima Literary Society
  • 1933 - Posted to Catholic Seminary at Amissano.
  • 1935 - Enrolled in Lincoln University, USA. He worked as a dishwasher to support his upkeep.
  • 1939 - Had his Bachelor's degree in Economics and Sociology
  • 1944 - Played a major role in the Pan-African Conference which made the US to help ensure Africa became independent.
  • 1945 - Enrolled in London School of Business
  • 1949 - Formed the Convention People's Party to overthrow the British from Ghana.
  • 1957 - Gained independence for Gold Coast
  • 1966 - Upon his visit to North Vietnam and China, he was overthrown and never came back to the country./li>
  • 1971 - Fled to Bucharest, Romania to seek for medical assistance
  • 1972 - Died of prostrate cancer at the age of 62.
